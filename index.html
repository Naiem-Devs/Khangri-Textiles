40 years of experience

At Khangri we partner with the finest craftsmen and artisans in the region, all of whom carry decades worth of experience. The knowledge and craftsmanship passed through generations to our artisans enable us to create work of the highest grade and quality at prices that are affordable and competitive.

Quality Assurance

Khangri Standards

All our items must meet our stringent quality standards to ensure they are of the highest grade and of premium quality. We employ rigorous checks to always meet the demands and expectations fo our clients.

Eco-conscious

Our products are made through eco-conscious methods. Our Swiss dyestuffs are eco-friendly and chemical free and our cashmere wool is 100% all natural. We ensure that all wastage can be reused or recycled.

Premium Materials

We only use the highest quality materials for our products. Our cashmere for example, comes from the rare Chyangra Himalyan goat, which produces the finest cashmere wool.

Generational Experience

Our items are individually handmade and hand-dyed by expert craftsmen who use traditional artisanal handloom techniques that have been passed down through generations from around the region.

How it Works

01.

Send our team your designs with the required specifications and dimensions needed for a sample. Any samples will require a partial fee.

02.

Our team then oversees the sampling process to ensure that our clients receive exactly what was specified. Quotes will also be provided.

03.

All samples are then sent for client approval prior to full production. Any edits to samples can still be made at this stage of production.

04.

Once the samples have been approved by the client, a timeline and final price will be given based on order quantity and delivery dates.
